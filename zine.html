The Meaning of Love

a zine project

The Meaning of Love is an independent zine project, made with InDesign, of a collection of personal writing and images. The zine delves into the ideas of friendship and love and its many intersecting qualities. While most of the content comes from my hometown in Los Angeles, the inclusion of imagery and thoughts from my time in San Francisco alludes to the ever-changing nature of friendship and the growth that occurs with meeting new people.
