Hello! My name is Erika Baumgartner and I was born and raised in Los Angeles, CA.

I am currently in my third year studying design at the University of San Francisco. Aside from design, I enjoy listening to music, playing guitar, and writing!
One of the things I value most while designing and making art is experimentation. Even though I was primarily drawing and painting growing up, I was still delving into different mediums as a way to further my understanding of art. As time passed, and I cemented myself into the world of more traditional art, I found myself wanting to stray away and start moving into the digital realm. From there, I began experimenting with different applications, which led me to delving into different forms of design that I would have never thought to engage with, like web design, printed design, and typography.
I believe that one of the best ways to grow as an artist, and as a human, is to jump in and learn along the way. There’s nothing better than screwing around, and discovering different ways to achieve something that you can be proud of in the end. My goal is to continue experimenting and learning more ways to create art that I’m proud to show, whether it’s online or tangible.
