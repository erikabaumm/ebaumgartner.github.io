Contrast and Inversion

experimenting with multiple mediums

One of my favorite things to experiment with in my art is contrast. I find that I used to be scared of high contrast, so I’d lower brightnesses and make colors within my pieces more muted. While my older work was still interesting despite the lower contrast, I decided to start making my work the opposite of what I was doing for a while. My biggest inspiration came from inverted photos, which I adore. While experimenting with photography and trying to create my own inverted imagery, I realized that I’d only be successful and achieve the look I wanted if I upped the contrast within my work. Once I started implementing that into my photography, I started seeing changes in the way I drew and performed with my other projects. This collection of images are experimental drawings, both traditional and digital, with variations of higher contrast and inverted colors.
