Logo Project

creating a logo for designer Muriel Cooper

This project was given as a way to become more accustomed to Adobe Illustrator and to combine research with artistic application. I was given the task to create a logo for Muriel Cooper, who was a digital designer, researcher, and educator. She was known for her time at MIT as well as her book and exhibition designs. I created a logo with an asterisk as the base, inspired by her Asterisk exhibition project, as well as her initials which are reminiscent of the logo she created for the MIT Press.Â
